# AI Risk Assessment Assistant

Final project for the Building AI course.

## Summary

AI Risk Assessment Assistant is an intelligent system that helps individuals and organizations identify potential risks before they become serious problems. It provides simple risk assessments and practical recommendations to improve planning and decision-making.

## Background

Many organizations face financial, operational, and security risks that are difficult to identify early. Poor risk management can lead to financial losses, delays, and safety issues.

This project aims to:
- Detect potential risks.
- Evaluate the level of risk.
- Suggest preventive actions.
- Improve decision-making.

## How is it used?

The user enters information about a project, business, or activity. The AI analyzes the information and produces:
- A list of possible risks.
- A risk level (Low, Medium, or High).
- Recommendations to reduce or avoid those risks.

This system could be useful for:
- Small businesses.
- Government organizations.
- Students.
- Project managers.

## Data sources and AI methods

Possible data sources include:
- Historical project reports.
- Risk management databases.
- Public safety reports.
- Organization policies.

AI methods that could be used:
- Machine Learning.
- Natural Language Processing (NLP).
- Classification algorithms.

## Challenges

This project is only designed to support decision-making. It cannot replace professional experts or guarantee that every possible risk will be identified.

## What next?

Future improvements include:
- Support for multiple languages.
- Integration with business management systems.
- More accurate AI prediction models.
- Real-time risk monitoring.
